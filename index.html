FAQ

Questions before you spin the web?

How does Spyda differ from standard AI generators?

Spyda doesn't just generate from scratch. It mathematically dissects a reference flyer, extracts its DNA (ingredients and structure), and allows you to seamlessly replace those elements to generate a custom, high-end professional design.

What are Credits and how are they used?

Credits are our compute currency. Every action has a specific cost based on the processing power required. For example, a flyer analysis costs 2 credits, while generating a standard design costs 5.

Do you copy the original reference design?

No. Spyda studies the structure, layout, and style direction of the reference. You inject your own text, images, and brand constants to generate an entirely fresh and legally distinct design.

How do Brand Constants work?

Brand Constants ensure your generations stay on-brand. You can define global colors, typography, and mood boards in your Brand Card. Spyda will automatically apply these rules when rendering your final design.

Can I remove the Spyda watermark?

Yes. Exports are watermarked on the Free tier. By upgrading to any paid plan (Basic, Creator, or Pro), watermarks are completely removed and you unlock High-Definition exports.

Are my uploaded flyers and generated designs private?

Absolutely. Your uploaded references and ingredient maps are private to your workspace. Team-level users can securely share their ingredient maps and assets for collaborative production.
