FAQ

Questions before you spin the web?

Does Spyda copy the flyer?

No. Spyda studies the structure and style direction so users can replace the ingredients and generate a fresh design.

Can I change text and images?

Yes. Text becomes editable fields, images become upload slots, and global style constants stay inside the Brand Card.

Which model creates the output?

Spyda uses GPT-Image 2 for generation, with Groq available for fast analysis and prompt intelligence.
